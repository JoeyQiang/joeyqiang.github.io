acuvue Caring your eyes efficiently and effectively
Acuvue is a WeChat based Web APP that helps users to get online support efficiently, such as Q&A, online shopping, and online redemption, which assists users to care their eyes anytime anywhere, while enhancing Acuvue’s brand awareness and promoting its products sale.

Analysis of Last Version

IA

Visual IA

  • A1 & A2 Instructions

    Cons:

    • Instructions should be question-centered instead of answer-centered
    • A answer opens up in a new web page after clicking the button
    • This page lacks connections with other pages

  • A3 Products

    Cons:

    • Big images cause slow loading
    • Annotations are too outstanding and cause confusion
    • The top menu is not clear as if it is clickable
    • The Registration part requires too much information

  • B1 Store Finder

    Cons:

    • Advertisements do not add value to the user experience
    • Location Stores function is not at the hero area
    • Map View feature is hidden in default state

  • B2 Free Sample

    Cons:

    • A hero image doesn’t necessarily add value, as the page straightly jumps to below form
    • Too many form fields for user to fill

  • B3 Coupon

    Cons:

    • The page straightly jumps to the form, completely skipping the coupons. Users has to scroll back
    • The heavy image is annoying and redundant

  • C1 Points Store

    Cons:

    • Categoris of products format are not clear for users
    • The registration notification does not directly lead user to the registration page

  • C2 Gift Redemption

    Cons:

    • The Banner image does not add value
    • The exchange process needs to be streamlined

  • C3 Points Rule

    Cons:

    • The title of a member card is unnecessary for unauthenticated users
    • Points information is hard to understand

  • C4 Admin Register

    Cons:

    • The function of admin registration should not be viewed by users
    • The error prompt does not instruct users what should to do next

  • C5 User Register

    Cons:

    • The registration form is too redundant
    • The title of the page does not make sense after users finish registration

Summary

Overall Findings Recommendations
Pages load slowly due to heavy images Optimize browse experience
The structure of information is not well organised Refine the IA via persona and user journey
Some key functions such asregistration, Redeeming points, is too fussy Simplify redundant procedures and push more accurate messages
Visual design needs to be improved and pages lack connection with each other Make design consistent among the APP to build a sense of accountability

Persona

Amber

First Time User
  • Know little about the contacts
  • Need to wear contacts for special occasions
  • Never use ACUVUE lenses or any other brand lenses

John

Experienced User
  • Have been using ACUVUE contacts
  • Never use ACUVUE WeChat account
  • Need to purchase contacts regularly

Linda

Loyal User
  • Wear ACUVUE contacts everyday
  • Have been using ACUVUE WeChat account
  • Be used to purchase ACUVUE contacts regularly

User Journey

Amber

First Experience

  • None

Offline

  • A clerk gives Amber an optometry and choose the contacts that fit her requirements
  • A clerk recommends the WeChat official account to Amber

Online

  • Amber follows the official account then registers to get points and coupons
  • She can use the points to redeem gifts
  • Also she can browse products and discover surrounding stores

John

First Experience

  • John finds out he will get more coupons and benefits if he registers as a member of ACUVUE
  • He follows the account then registers

Offline

  • John comes to the store to buy the contacts with a coupon
  • He knows from the clerk that the account can also accumulate points

Online

  • John uses the points to redeem gifts
  • He tries free samples before he buys contacts
  • He starts to pay attentions to promotion activities posted online

Linda

First Experience

  • Linda runs out of her contacts and she needs to buy some more
  • She uses the Find Stores function from the official account to locate stores

Offline

  • Linda buys the contacts then ask a clerk how many points she can get
  • She asks a clerk that are there any new free samples she can try

Online

  • Linda uses points to redeem gifts
  • She pays attention to all new discounts
  • She also pays attention to all new-released products and is glad to try them

Updated UX

AI

Visual AI

A1 Products List

Improvements:

  • Change the link of free sample and coupon to the attractive button, call to action
  • Classify coupons to limited and unlimited ones, motivating users to use coupon asap
  • Keep all products within one page, which is more friendly for user to browse. And put Free Sample as a banner at the bottom of the page

A2 Guide

Improvements:

  • Add question-related products recommendation with each question, increasing the selling conversion rate
  • Keep details of the answer on the same page with the question, which can be collapsed and expanded

B1 Find Stores

Improvements:

  • Use Map View to give users a clear and straightforward finding experience
  • Show surrounding stores in default based on user’s location, and also provide search function. Put the nearest store's detail information on the Find Store page
  • After users select the store, they can view its information and use WeChat Route API to navigate

B2 Coupons

Improvements:

  • Show coupons that users already have
  • Send users coupons from time to time, and prompt them the message when they land on the page
  • Give users a getting coupon link at the bottom of the page if clients want to collect some information from users

C1 Points & Redemption

Improvements:

  • Reorganize the content, give a bottom link jumping to Getting Points Page
  • Add progress bar to each product, prompting users how far they can exchange the product and encouraging users to get points
  • After exchange a product successfully, users can view redemption history on the account page

C2 My Account

Improvements:

  • Change the page name to the easily-understood one
  • The first time when users land on the page, ask users to sign up so they can use 100 free points
  • Add Coupons Information & Redemption History to the account page after users sign up
