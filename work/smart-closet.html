smart closet Making dressing and storage seamlessly interactive
The smart closet helps users dress correctly, effectively, seamlessly and look their best, by making suggestions depending on their daily schedule, context and conditions. It will also give them control over their items, purchases, donations, borrow-lend-return history and overall inventory of their storage spaces

Storyboard

Our objective is to provide a new way to dress and store clothing items to make users interact with daily dressing activities naturally and seamlessly

2. What could happen?

What users need to do

  • Wear inappropriate clothes due to: not remembering activity, lack of time, change of schedule, etc

What users need to know

  • Not knowing what to wear due to: lack of memory, not having a storage code, misplacing, etc

How analytics get answers

  • Not analyzing it properly due to: lack of time, no interest, stuffed closet, no help from others, etc

What make this possible

  • Don’t care to organize 
  • Different ways of storage code 
  • Have a wrong hunch on: time, location, combination, organization

3. What should happen?

What users need to do

  • Spend no time thinking of what to wear 
  • Choose from suggestions
  • Locate items quickly

What users need to know

  • How to operate the system
  • Interface interacts with  storage infrastructure 

How analytics get answers

  • Easily-understood interface is the helper
  • Knowledge of: organization codes, items’ location, clothes care, dress based on shape, buy if needed

What make this possible

  • A seamless interaction with a dressing-storage system

Focus Audience

Real Estate Developers

In the early stage, real estate developers can dictate the guidelines of the technology and systems that would be required in their projects

Closet Retailers

Having closet retailers as partners will direct the smart closet system into other areas, expanding its purpose from store clothes to other types of services, such as renting and buying clothes

Independent Home Owners

Having close communication with homeowners and their needs, will keep us updated and in sink with trend changes and new market demands
