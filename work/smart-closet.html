smart closet Making dressing and storage seamlessly interactive
The smart closet system will help our users dress correctly, effectively, seamlessly and look their best, by making suggestions depending on their daily schedule, context and conditions. It will also give them control over their items, purchases, donations, borrow-lend-return history and overall inventory of their storage spaces.

Storyboard & Objective

Our objective is to provide a new way to dress and store clothing items to make our users interaction with daily dressing activities natural and seamless.

Focus Audience

Real Estate Developers

Commercial Lines: In the early stage of, real estate developers can dictate the guidelines of the technology and systems that would be required in their projects.

Closet Retailers

Research, Testing and Innovation: Having closet retailers as partners will direct the smart closet system into other areas, expanding its purpose from clothing to other types of storage.

Independent Home Owners

Custom Line: Having close communication with homeowners and their needs to have a custom product, will keep us updated and in sink with trend changes and new market demands.
