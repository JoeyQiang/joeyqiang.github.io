inplace Connecting seniors with loving community
InPlace is a service exchange mobile APP that connects providers with those in need of services. This solution is designed to bring a shared sense of community to our seniors who want to share accumulated resources such as time, presence, and knowledge, with recipients looking for personal enrichment.

Research & Insights

  • Be easy to use, intuitive, through a minimalist and customizable interface
  • Give users, older adults in particular, a sense of autonomy
  • Include and assist users in the co-collaboration of the match-making process
  • Allow for users to search database for various types of value or resources within the local community
  • Give users up-to-date notifications and will support users to be notified and confirm exchange
  • Give users the ability to rate their experience to better match them with future providers and support reciprocal exchanges
