inplace connecting seniors with loving community
InPlace is a service exchange mobile app that connects providers with those in need of services. This solution is designed to bring a shared sense of community to our seniors who want to share accumulated resources such as time, presence and knowledge, with recipients looking for personal enrichment.

Research & Insights

  • Be easy to use, intuitive, through a minimalist and customizable interface.
  • Give our users, older adults in particular, a sense of autonomy.
  • Include and assist our users in the co-collaboration of the matchmaking process.
  • Allow for users to search our database for various types of value or resources within the local community.
  • Give our users up-to-date notifications and will support users to be notified and confirm exchange.
  • Give our users the ability to rate their experience and give feedback to better match them with future providers and support reciprocal exchanges.
