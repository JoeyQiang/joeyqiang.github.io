wechat now cms Customizing your WeChat Account and unleash its potential
WECHAT NOW CMS is a customized WeChat Account backend management system developed which support customized design. Clients can create different features using the system, which provides the most convenient way to help both themselves and users to achieve goals.

Approach

  • Base CMS features set up
  • Define clients’ sepecific objectives
  • Add user-centered features accordingly
  • Explore specific custom APIs
  • Keep absolute flexibility for integration
  • Flexible data analytics

Features & UX

Dashboard

The dashboard is the central admin panel where administrator can access the different data collected by CMS, such as the total number of followers, the broadcast views of groups, the ranks ofQR campaign. Clients can customize the dashboard page based on their hobby.

Tags Management

Track the interest of followers by adding tags to published contents. Followers' behaviour can be defined by their actions with contents (view, like or share).

Group Segmentation

Create and manage followers by different segmentation types: gender, age, region, language, following time, active time, tags, and QR labels.

Targeted Broadcast Scheduling

Reach and engage certain groups by sending customized news. Moreover, schedule broadcasts ahead of time to specific groups.

POI Finder

Followers can find interested places around them in two interaction modes. One is sending your location to the backend to get a POI list. The other is tabbing a menu item to jump a POI H5 page.

QR Campaign

Create campaigns to track users that scanned the QR code attached with the campaign. Then the backend can analyze campaigns’ data and show the result in the Analytics module.

Conclusion

  • Make your design satisfied with business goals. Identify what key functions can be sold easily. Keep your design process in a balance among time, money and quality.
  • Make your design friendly for developers. Try to find the easiest way to acheive a function in technical aspect. Discuss with developers together in the preliminary process and try to design interfaces using existed frontend framework widgets.
  • Make your design accountable for the team. Review your design with your superior, co-workers, and even friends. Keep all your changes in a log and save a copy every time. Last, practice your presentation to make your design persuasive & credible.
