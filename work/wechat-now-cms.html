wechat now cms customizing your WeChat Account and unleash its potential
WECHAT NOW CMS is a customized WeChat Account backend management system which can swtich the same official account to custom mode. Clients can create different features using the system, which provides the most convinient way to help both themselves and users to achieve goals.

Apporach

  • Base CMS features set up
  • Define client’s sepecific objectives
  • Add user centric features accordingly supported by CMS
  • Explore specific custom APIs
  • Keep absolute flexibility for integration

Features & UX

Dashboard

The dashboard is your central admin panel where you can access the different features proposed by WeChat Now CMS, such as the amount of followers, the broadcast views of groups, the QR campaign ranks and more than can be customized depending on your customized WeChat Services.

Tags Management

Track the interest of your users by adding tags to your content. Their behaviour is defined by their actions with articles (viewing the content).

Group Segmentation

Create and manage your followers by different segmentation types: gender, age, region, language, following time, active time, active time, tags and QR labels.

Targeted Broadcast Scheduling

Reach and engage your community by sending daily customised news. Also, boradcast to specific groups created and schedule your broadcast ahead of time.

POI Finder

Followers can find interested places around them in two interaction modes. One is sending your location to backend to get a POI list (category) back. The other one is by clicking a menu list item to jump a POI H5 page (collection).

QR Campaign

Create your own campaign to track users that scanned the QR code you created. You can assign content or url to be linked to it. Then backend can analyze campaign’s data to show them on Analytics module.

Conclusion

  • Make your design satisfy business goal. Identify what are key functions can be sold easitestly. Keep your design process on a balance among time, money and quality.
  • Make your design friendly for developers. Try to find the easiest way to acheive a function on technical aspect. Discuss with developers together in the preliminary process and try to design interfaces using existed frontend framework widgets.
  • Make your design accountable for team. Review your design with your superior, co-workers, PM and even friends. Keep all your changes in log and always save a copy of the last version. Practice your presentation to meke your design ideas credible & persuasive.
